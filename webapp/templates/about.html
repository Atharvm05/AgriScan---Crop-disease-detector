About AgriScan

Empowering farmers with AI-powered crop disease detection technology

Our Mission

AgriScan aims to revolutionize crop disease management by providing farmers with an accessible, easy-to-use tool that can identify plant diseases instantly, even in areas with limited or no internet connectivity. By combining advanced machine learning with practical agricultural knowledge, we help farmers take timely action to protect their crops and improve yields.

How It Works

Take a Photo

Capture an image of the plant leaf showing signs of disease using your device's camera or upload an existing image.

AI Analysis

Our machine learning model analyzes the image to identify the disease affecting the plant with high accuracy.

Get Recommendations

Receive detailed information about the disease and actionable treatment recommendations.

The Technology

AgriScan uses a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) based on MobileNetV2 architecture, trained on the PlantVillage dataset containing thousands of images of healthy and diseased plant leaves. The model is optimized for mobile devices using TensorFlow Lite, allowing it to run efficiently on smartphones and tablets without requiring an internet connection.

Model Architecture

MobileNetV2-based CNN optimized for mobile devices

Training Dataset

PlantVillage dataset with various crop disease images

Offline Capability

TensorFlow Lite for on-device inference without internet

Supported Crops and Diseases

AgriScan can identify diseases in various crops including:

Tomato

  • Early Blight
  • Late Blight
  • Leaf Mold
  • Septoria Leaf Spot
  • Spider Mites
  • Target Spot
  • Yellow Leaf Curl Virus
  • Mosaic Virus
  • Bacterial Spot

Potato

  • Early Blight
  • Late Blight
  • Healthy

Apple

  • Apple Scab
  • Black Rot
  • Cedar Apple Rust
  • Healthy

Corn (Maize)

  • Cercospora Leaf Spot (Gray Leaf Spot)
  • Common Rust
  • Northern Leaf Blight
  • Healthy

Note: The actual diseases detected may vary based on the specific model training.

Privacy and Data Usage

We respect your privacy. When using the offline mode, all image processing happens directly on your device, and no data is sent to external servers. Images uploaded in online mode are temporarily stored for processing and are automatically deleted after analysis.
